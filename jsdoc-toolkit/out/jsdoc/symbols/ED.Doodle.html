ED.Doodle(_drawing, _originX, _originY, _apexX, _apexY, _scaleX, _scaleY, _arc, _rotation, _order)
Doodles are components of drawings which have built in knowledge of what they represent, and how to behave when manipulated; Doodles are drawn in the 'doodle plane' consisting of 1001 pixel square grid with central origin (ie -500 to 500) and are rendered in a canvas element using a combination of the affine transform of the host drawing, and the doodle's own transform.
